Danao Adventure Park

Danao Adventure Park is nestled in a scenic mountainous area overlooking the Wahig River. It’s known for offering eco-tourism and adrenaline-pumping adventures in a sustainable way, all while highlighting Bohol’s natural beauty and cultural heritage.

How to Get There



From Tagbilaran:


  • Drive or ride a van/bus to Danao (approx. 2–3 hours)

  • It's best to rent a vehicle or join a tour for convenience

Entrance Fee



  • The entrance fee to Danao Adventure Park ranges from ₱40 to ₱60 per person, depending on whether you're visiting for the day or staying overnight.



    • facilities



  • Basic accommodation (eco-lodges and dorm-type rooms)

  • Function halls for team building or educational seminars

  • A small restaurant serving local food

    • Why Visit Danao Adventure Park



    If you're looking for adventure, nature, and culture all in one place, Danao Adventure Park is a must-visit destination in Bohol. Tucked away in the highlands of Danao, this eco-tourism spot offers experiences you won’t find anywhere else on the island.

    Thrill-Seeker’s Playground



    Home to The Plunge, a 200-meter canyon swing, and one of the highest and scariest in the Philippines—this park is paradise for adrenaline junkies. Add ziplining, caving, rappelling, and river kayaking for a full day of action.

    Immersive Nature Experience



    Surrounded by lush forest and overlooking the Wahig River, it’s the perfect escape into nature. Whether you’re gliding over the trees or hiking through caves, the views are always incredible.

    Eco + Educational Tourism



    Danao isn’t just about the thrills. The park promotes sustainable tourism and offers insights into local farming practices, environmental preservation, and even Boholano heritage.

    Great for Groups & Team Building




    With its open spaces, obstacle courses, and group packages, it’s an ideal venue for barkadas, families, and company outings.

    Stay Overnight in the Mountains



    Want a unique mountain getaway? Stay in their simple eco-lodges and enjoy a peaceful night away from the city—just you, the stars, and the cool Danao air.

    Things to do in Danao Adventure Park

    The Plunge

    Not for the faint-hearted! Experience a 75-meter freefall swing over a deep gorge—perfect for adrenaline junkies. It's one of the most extreme rides in the country!

    Zipline Adventure


    Soar across a canyon and enjoy panoramic views of the forest and river below. This is one of the park's most popular and scenic rides.

    Wall Climbing & Rappelling

    Challenge your strength and courage as you climb up or rappel down cliff faces surrounded by nature.

    River Kayaking



    Paddle through the calm stretches of the Wahig River—ideal for those who want a more relaxed yet fun water activity.

    Caving (Kamira Cave)

    Go spelunking in Kamira Cave and discover underground rivers, rock formations, and a bit of Bohol’s hidden beauty.

    Sky Ride




    A scenic cable ride across the canyon, perfect for guests who want the view without the thrill.

    Eco Tours



    Learn about sustainable practices, local farming techniques, and conservation efforts in the area.

    Overnight Stays in Eco-Lodges

    Spend the night surrounded by nature. Wake up to the sound of birds and fresh mountain air.

    Group Activities & Team Building

    Perfect for companies, schools, or big families—complete with obstacle courses and team challenges.
