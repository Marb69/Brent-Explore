Virgin Island Island

Virgin Island (also known as Pungtud or Pontod Island) is probably the most iconic island destinations in Bohol. Its main draw: a lengthy sandbar that curls and fades into the sea like the tail of a mischievous tadpole (or a sperm cell; there, we said it). The sandy runway makes for a postcard perfect backdrop! The surrounding areas are mostly grassy, though.

Best Time to Visit Virgin Island

The best time to visit Bohol, and therefore Balicasag and Virgin Island, is during the dry season, which runs from November to May. During these months, you can expect sunny skies, calm seas, and pleasant temperatures.

Things to Do in Virgin Island



  • Snorkeling: The waters around Bohol Virgin Island are crystal clear and teeming with marine life.


  • Island Hopping: Bohol Virgin Island is just one of the many islands in the area that are worth exploring.



  • Beach Volleyball: If you're traveling with friends or family, why not organize a friendly game of beach volleyball?


    • How to Get to Virgin Island?

    • Fly to Panglao Airport or take a ferry from Cebu City to Bohol.
    • Many resorts and tour operators in Tagbilaran and Panglao Island offer tours around these islands. Boat rental costs from P1500-P2500.


    You Dont have missed

    Coconut and banana que (fried bananas on stick) vendors offer delicious merienda on the island! If you’re feeling a little adventurous, try eating raw sea urchins (minus the spines, of course). You can spot the vendors easily if they haven’t welcomed you to the island upon docking yet.
