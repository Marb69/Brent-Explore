Dimiao Twin Falls

Pahangog Falls (also called Dimiao Twin Falls) is located in Dimiao on the island of Bohol in the Philippines. The twin waterfalls cascade over a 30-meter cliff surrounded by lush forest before falling into a natural basin.

    • Things to do in Dimiao twin Falls

    Dimiao Twin Falls, also known as Pahangog Falls, offers a variety of activities, including swimming, jumping from rocks, and taking photos. The trek to the falls involves a steep descent and is considered a physically demanding journey. You can also enjoy the scenery of the surrounding hills, rice fields, palm trees, and tropical forests along the way.

    Best time to go in Dimiao twin Falls

    The best time to visit Dimiao Twin Falls is during the dry season, which is from November to April. During this time, the weather is sunny and dry, which makes it perfect for outdoor activities like hiking and swimming.
