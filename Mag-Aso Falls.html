Mag-aso Falls

Mag-Aso Falls is an 8-meter waterfall that is surrounded by a tropical backdrop of tall trees and wild plants. Carved out under the waterfall is a natural pool that is particularly inviting to go for a swim due to its milky blue-colored water derived from the limestone rocks in the area.

How to go in Mag-aso Falls



  • At Tagbilaran City, catch a jeep at Cogon Market to Antequera, fare is P25/person

  • Transfer to a single motorcycle at P25/person (P50/tricycle) one-way, to take you to Mag-aso Falls.

  • Take the 197 steps going to the waterfalls.

Best time to travel in Mag-aso Falls



Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Mag-Aso Falls is during the dry season (November to May) when the water is clear, and the flow is moderate and not too powerful.
