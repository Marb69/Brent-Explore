Ingkumhan Falls

Ingkumhan Falls is one of the best waterfalls in Bohol and a must-see while you are adventuring around the island. Cliff jumps and rope swings are just some of the reasons that make this Bohol tourist attraction one of the top locations to visit!

How go to Ingkumhan Falls




  • The best way to get to Ingkumhan Falls in Bohol is to rent a scooter from one of the many scooter rental stores dotted around Panglao.

  • Currently, there are no Bohol tours that I know of which include these falls on the itinerary so you will have to find your own way to this location.



Things to do in Ingkumhan Falls


Cliff jumping

Rope swinging








Visitor Information on Ingkumhan Falls

Entrance Fee

  • Php 20 per person

  • Additional fees may apply for parking or use of cottages.

    • Best Time to Visit

  • Early morning to noon for the best lighting and fewer visitors.
  • Best visited during the dry season (November to May) for clear waters and safer trails.
