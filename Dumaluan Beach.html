Dumaluan Beach Resort

Escape to the tranquil Dumaluan Beach Resort on Panglao Island, Bohol. This beachfront haven offers a serene retreat for two travelers seeking a slice of paradise. Lounge on the pristine white sand beach or take a dip in the refreshing swimming pool. Indulge in delicious Filipino and international cuisine at the on-site restaurant. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and enjoy stunning views from your private balcony. Explore nearby landmarks like Dumaluan Beach (571 meters) and Tarsier Botanika (1872 meters). Experience the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure at Dumaluan Beach Resort.

Best Time to Visit dumaluan Beach

    November to April

  • Dry months (November to April): Ideal for beach activities and outdoor exploration.
  • Local festivals: A vibrant time to experience the culture and community spirit.
  • Off-peak season: For those seeking quieter moments, visiting just before or after the peak season can be perfect.

How to Get to dumaluan Beach?



From Tagbiliran

By Car or Taxi:

  • Travel time: Around 30–40 minutes

  • Distance: Approx. 20 km

  • Just head south to Panglao Island via the Dauis–Panglao Road. Once on Panglao, follow signs to Dumaluan Beach (it's near the more famous Alona Beach area).

By Tricycle:



  • Cost: Around ₱300–₱500 (one way, depending on negotiation)

  • Travel time: 40–50 minutes

  • Tricycles can be found at the port or city center.

By Van or Multicab (Shared):


  • Look for vans or multicabs bound for Panglao.

  • Let the driver know you're heading to Dumaluan Beach, and they can drop you off near the entrance.

  • More affordable but might take a bit longer due to multiple stops.


From Panglao International Airport:

By Car or Taxi:

  • Travel time: Around 15–20 minutes
  • Distance: About 8–10 km

By Tricycle:

  • Cost: Around ₱150–₱300
  • Travel time: Around 20–25 minutes

Things to Do in dumaluan Beach

  • Swimming & Sunbathing: The calm, shallow waters make it ideal for a relaxing dip or just lounging by the shore.



  • Snorkeling: Rent gear and explore the underwater life near the beach or join a tour to nearby snorkeling spots.



  • Banana Boat & Jet Ski:For a dose of excitement, you can rent these from the resort or nearby vendors.



  • Island Hopping Tours:Dumaluan is a great jump-off point for trips to Balicasag Island, Virgin Island, and Pamilacan Island.



  • Beach Volleyball or Frisbee: The wide beach offers plenty of space for some light sports and group games.



  • Sunset Watching: The beach faces west, so expect amazing sunsets, especially with a cold drink in hand.
